Product Shot


Project Info -

I shot a range of different objects during my time spent on this assignment. I took photographs of a camera, makeup and perfume bottles. The perfume bottles really appealed to me and I chose to focus on that for my chosen image. I took shots of the perfume “Alien” by Thierry Mugler. I used a background light behind the perfume bottle and had the main light in front of it at a slight angle. I covered the background light with paper, to diffuse it. My shutter speed was 1/125, the ISO was set to 100 and the aperture was F11. At first I found it difficult to overcome the reflection and shine off the liquid and glass bottle. After some research and light alterations it began to work out better.
